About snake.io

snake.io is a fun and addictive online multiplayer game where you control a snake, grow longer by eating pellets, and compete against players worldwide. The goal is to become the largest snake on the leaderboard.

How to Play

Use your mouse or touch screen to navigate your snake. Avoid colliding with other snakes, and strategically outmaneuver your opponents to survive and grow.

Features

  • Multiplayer gameplay in real-time.
  • Simple controls, easy to learn.
  • Responsive design for PC and mobile devices.
  • Fun, competitive, and addictive.
