Burger Boss

A fun restaurant simulation game where you build and manage your burger empire!

About Burger Boss

Burger Boss is an addictive restaurant simulation game where you manage every aspect of your burger business. Hire staff, cook burgers, serve customers, and expand your restaurant empire to become the ultimate burger tycoon.

How to Play

Prepare and serve burgers as quickly as possible. Upgrade your kitchen, hire employees, and manage your resources wisely to satisfy more customers and earn higher profits.

Game Features

  • Fun and engaging restaurant simulation gameplay
  • Build and upgrade your burger empire
  • Hire staff and manage operations
  • Free to play directly in your browser
  • Responsive design for PC and mobile devices
