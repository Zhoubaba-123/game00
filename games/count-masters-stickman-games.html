About Count Masters: Stickman Games

Count Masters: Stickman Games is a fun, fast-paced casual running game where you control a group of stickmen. Navigate through obstacles, collect more stickmen, and race against other players to become the ultimate winner.

How to Play

Use your mouse or touchscreen to steer your stickmen. Collect additional stickmen along the path to increase your group, avoid obstacles, and reach the finish line before your opponents. Timing and strategy are key!

Game Features

  • Exciting stickman running gameplay
  • Collect and grow your stickman group
  • Multiple obstacle-filled levels
  • Compete in real-time races
  • Playable instantly online on PC and mobile
